Our Story

Crafted with
Purpose

Inkwell was born from a simple belief: the tools you use shape the ideas you create. We curate stationery that inspires, not just functions — right from the heart of India.

2019
Year Founded

From a Jaipur Room to 18,000+ Happy Customers

It all started in a small room in Jaipur — Aanya Sharma, a former architect, was tired of ordering stationery from abroad and waiting weeks for delivery. She began sourcing beautiful handmade notebooks from local artisans in Sanganer, a village near Jaipur famous for its hand-block printing and paper craft.

What began as a weekend passion project — selling on Instagram to friends and family — quickly caught on. People loved the quality, the packaging, and the personal touch. Within six months, Inkwell had 2,000 customers across India.

Today, we work with over 40 independent makers and brands — from paper mills in Tamil Nadu to pen craftsmen in Rajasthan, and calligraphy artisans from West Bengal. We bring India's finest stationery to your doorstep, wherever you are in the country.
